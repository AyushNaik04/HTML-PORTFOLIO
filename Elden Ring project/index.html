Queen Marika and the lands between

Queen Marika is a god and the current vessel of the Elden Ring. She reigns as the divine sovereign of the Lands Between, and her Demigod offspring are shardbearers in possession of the Great Runes. She has been missing for quite some time. For more info click here

The lands between

"Lands Between" is a term from the video game Elden Ring, referring to the game's world. It's a vast and diverse landmass with varied biomes, from lush grasslands to a frozen, mountainous north, all dominated by the Erdtree. The name "Lands Between" is intentionally ambiguous, meant to evoke a sense of mystery and serve as an invitation to explore this new world.

Ranni the witch

Ranni the Witch, also known as Lunar Princess Ranni and Renna the Witch, is a character in Elden Ring. She is one of the Demigods, and the focus of one of the game's most elaborate questlines.

Godfrey the First Elden Lord

Godfrey is a former consort of Queen Marika the Eternal and the first Elden Lord of the Golden Order.[1][2] Although his actions directly shaped much of the history of the Lands Between, Queen Marika stole his grace and banished him to the badlands.
